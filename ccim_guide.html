GUIDE: Character Collection for Injustice Mobile




This is the guide for Character Collection for Injustice Mobile.

What You Can Do with the Character Collection Spreadsheet

  1. Track your Character Collection, saving it to a file, the server, or both
  2. Ask others for help with building your team, and help others with building their teams
  3. Get a list of all characters at any Elite and XP levels, with 0%, 50%, or 100% augmentations
  4. Erase the form and start over
  5. Sort and Filter the characters in your collection.

The following sections will describe how to do each of these things

Tracking Your Own Character Collection

How to enter Elite and XP for your character collection:

  1. In the "Tag:" field, type a name which will be easy for you to remember
  2. Fix the bonuses to match what you have in your collection:
    1. Click "Edit > Change Support Cards".
    2. In the popup, uncheck any support bonus cards you do not have.
    3. Check the ones you do have in your collection.
    4. When you are done, click "Apply"
  3. Change which columns are displayed on the form. To do this:
    1. Click "Preferences > Configure Columns".
    2. Remove all columns except for Elite and XP. If Elite and XP are not there, add them.
    3. Click "Apply Changes".
    By eliminating extraneous columns, it is easier to enter data.
  4. If your device is fast enough & your screen is tall enough, then you can increase the number of characters per page using "Preferences > Change #Characters per Page".
  5. Open the Injustice app on your device
  6. Sort the characters in the app by Name. Also sort the form by "Name"
  7. On the form, use the tab key (or mouse-click) to get to the Elite and XP fields of each Character you have, then enter the Elite and XP values of your cards.
  8. When you finish with the last character on the form, click on the "> 1" button to go forward to the next page of characters.
  9. Continue entering Elite and XP values for your Character Cards until you finish the last page.

Finally, SAVE YOUR WORK! Click on "File > Server: Save" (or "File > File: Save"). When you come back to the form, you will be able to use "File > Server: Load" (or "File > File: Load") to get your work back.

Here are the rest of the fields you can enter for each character, and where they appear in the Injustice app:

Damage
Appears under the blue fist on the back of the card
Health
Appears under the red heart
Special 1 Bars
Appears along the bottom of the character card
Special 2 Bars
Appears along the bottom of the character card, after Special 1 bars
Super Bars
Appears along the bottom of the character card, after Special 2 bars
Crit. Chance
Appears on the next line under "Damage" and "Health"
Crit. Damage
Appears on the next line under "Damage" and "Health", after Crit Chance
Gear Slots
Appears on the right side of the card, under the character's support cards. Enter a number 1 to 3
In Store
Tap "Store" to go to the store in the app. Tap the "Add Characters" and "Promote" tabs to see which cards are available in the store
Extra Cards
Tap "Store" to go to the store. Tap "Sell" tab to see which cards you have extra copies of.

To enter these values into the form:

  1. Sort the form the same way you sort the characters in the app
  2. Click "Preferences > Configure Columns" to display the columns you would like to edit
  3. In the app, tap the character card to see the back of it.
  4. On the form, click the field to be changed, and type in the value (usually a number). If you have a keyboard, the tab key may be helpful.
  5. Once again, do not forget to save your changes using "File > Server: Save" (or "File > File: Save")

Note that if you have already set the Elite and XP values for a character, the Damage and Health fields will already have values in them. If the Damage and/or Health values on the form do not match those on your cards in the app, you have probably applied Damage or Health augmentation cards in the app. When you change the fields on the form to match the values in the app, the Damage Augmentation Level and/or the Health Augmentation Level will be automatically recalculated.

Other columns for each character: some columns are constants (such as Cost) or are calculated (such as Augmentation Level). Here's a list of them:

Cost
Constant: Number of power credits needed to buy the character, if it is in the store, except for The Killing Joke Joker.
Base Damage
Constant: Starting Damage Stat when the character is first added to your collection at Elite 0, XP level 1
Base Health
Constant: Starting Health Stat when the character is at Elite 0, XP level 1
Resale
Calculated: 0.3 X Cost
Special 1 Min
Calculated, based on Damage and Number of Special 1 bars
Special 1 Max
Calculated, based on Damage and Number of Special 1 bars
Special 2 Min
Calculated, based on Damage and Number of Special 2 bars
Special 2 Max
Calculated, based on Damage and Number of Special 2 bars
Super
Calculated, based on Damage and Number of Super bars
Damage Augmentation Level
Calculated, based on Damage, Elite, and XP
Health Augmentation Level
Calculated, based on Health, Elite, and XP
Base Damage + Base Health
Calculated, the sume of Base Damage and Base Health
Damage + Health
Calculated, the sume of Damage and Health

If you have suggestions for additional input fields or calculated fields, please let me know.

Giving and Receiving Help with Team Building

To receive help with building your team:

  1. Enter your character collection in the spreadsheet form
  2. Enter a Tag on the form
  3. Use "File > Server: Save" to save your collection on the server.
  4. Note the date and time you saved it
  5. Go to the chat room for the Character Collection Spreadsheet and ask your question. Make sure you include the Tag and the date and time you saved your data
  6. Check back in the chat room to answers from the community

To give help to other community members in building their teams:

  1. Go to the chat room for the Character Collection Spreadsheet and read the questions there.
  2. If you want to help someone, answer their questions
  3. Also check out the other chat rooms.

Get a list of all characters at any Elite and XP levels

Let's suppose you want to see a list of all characters' Damage and Health at Elite 7, XP level 50, with maxed augmentations. Here's how you do it.

  1. If you were working on your own collection, save it to the server or a file
  2. Click "Select > Select All"
  3. Click "Edit > Change to maximum values ..."
  5. Select 100%
  6. If Damage or Health columns are not visible, click "Preferences > Configure Columns" and add them
  7. If you like, you can sort by Damage or Health

You can calculate 0% or 50% augmentation levels the same way.

You can calculate any other XP or Elite level using "Edit > Change Selected Characters"

Functionality

List of features / functions:
  1. When user clicks on most editable fields, a small popup appears showing the name (or type) of data to enter
  2. User can change the number of characters displayed per 'page'
  3. User can change which columns are shown for each character, and the column order
  4. If table is too wide, the columns become horizontally scrollable to the right of 'Name' and 'Title'
  5. Row color matches character's color (except for Name and Title, which are pinned to the left of the table)
  6. User can navigate to 1st page, last page, or back or forward several pages using the "<<" etc buttons at the bottom of the table
  7. Select and deselect characters individually, a page at a time, all of them, none of them, or only the 'visible' ones. Invert the selection.
  8. Hide the selected characters. Show all characters.
  9. Change all selected characters. Choose a field and the value to change them to.
  10. Sort the characters
  11. Filter the characters. Hides characters which do not meet the user-specified filters.
  12. Save data to file
  13. Load data from file
  14. Update support (bonus) cards, recalculate health, damage, etc
  15. Reset the collection and the filters back to blanks
  16. Switch between light and dark color themes
  17. Turn on/off switch to load filters from a save.
  18. Clickable links to Guide, Chat, Make Donation
  19. When certain columns are changed, other columns are recalculated. Example: augmentation levels are recalculated when Damage or Health values are updated by the user.
